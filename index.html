About Luntiang Agos Farm

Luntiang Agos Farm is a sustainable agricultural enterprise specializing in aquaponics and hydroponics farming systems. By integrating modern farming technology with environmentally friendly practices, we produce fresh, healthy vegetables and freshwater fish while conserving water and promoting food security in the community.

Who We Are

Luntiang Agos Farm combines fish culture and soil-less farming to produce high-quality vegetables and fish in a clean, efficient, and sustainable environment.

Our Commitment

We are committed to protecting the environment by reducing water consumption, minimizing waste, and encouraging climate-smart agriculture through innovative farming methods.

What We Offer

We provide fresh vegetables, healthy tilapia, farm consultation, aquaponics and hydroponics installation, training, workshops, and educational farm tours.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Aquaponics?

Aquaponics combines fish farming and hydroponics, allowing plants to receive nutrients naturally from fish while helping clean the water.

What is Hydroponics?

Hydroponics is a method of growing plants without soil, using nutrient-rich water to produce healthy crops.

Do you accept bulk orders?

Yes. We accommodate both retail and wholesale orders depending on product availability.

Can schools schedule educational tours?

Absolutely! Schools, universities, and organizations are welcome to book guided farm tours and demonstrations.

Do you install aquaponics systems?

Yes. We design and install customized aquaponics and hydroponics systems for homes, schools, and commercial farms.
